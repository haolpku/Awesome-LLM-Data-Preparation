# 📎 Related Surveys

> Back to [main README](../README.md)

This page lists other surveys adjacent to LLM data preparation, with a short note on where each focuses and why ours complements it.

---

- **Bai et al. (2024) — Survey on Multimodal Data Preparation for LLMs.**
  *Focus:* multimodal data specifically; strong reference for image/text/audio alignment.
  *Complement:* our survey is text-first and covers operator / workflow abstractions which are absent here.

- **Zha et al. (2023/2025) — Data-Centric AI: Perspectives and Challenges.**
  *Focus:* broad overview of data-centric AI across problems.
  *Complement:* we narrow to LLMs with up-to-date pipelines (2024–2025).

- **Zhou et al. (2025) — A Survey on Data-Centric LLMs.**
  *Focus:* recent data-centric LLM landscape.
  *Complement:* our survey adds a structured operator-workflow taxonomy and links datasets to their preparation recipes.

- **Wang et al. (2023) — Data Management for LLMs.**
  *Focus:* storage, access, and management aspects.
  *Complement:* we focus on *preparation algorithms and workflows*, not storage/retrieval systems.

- **Liu et al. (2024) — Datasets for LLMs: A Survey.**
  *Focus:* catalog of datasets.
  *Complement:* we catalog datasets **together with their preparation workflows** and situate them in a PT/CPT/Post-training taxonomy.

- **Data Preparation for Fine-Tuning (2025).**
  *Focus:* fine-tuning only.
  *Complement:* we cover the full pipeline from PT to RLHF, with consistent operator abstractions across stages.
